Wellness for builders is not luxury — it is maintenance. Scent, touch, and ritual can support recovery when used with realistic expectations. Nothing here replaces medical care.

A consult should cover health history, medications, scent preferences, home environment (pets, kids, room size), and realistic goals. You leave with dilution guidance and a small starter protocol — not a shopping cart of twelve bottles.

— JV · Dark Heart Labs.
