Homage is labor —
bowing without losing your spine.

Gratitude is the daily receipt:
what kept you,
what cost you,
what you still owe the living.

An homage without audit
is wallpaper.

Keep the spine.
Keep the list honest.
Let thanks be small enough to repeat
and large enough to matter.

The receipt fits in your pocket.
