Humanity is vast chorus —
flaws and strengths in every register,
goodwill the difficult harmony.

Civility is cornerstone, not costume.
Forbearance guides the hand.
Compassion is not softness.
It is infrastructure.

Difference reveals common ground
when sorting by surface is refused.

Shared destiny is labor:
world treasured by repair,
not by brochure.

Honor bonds that hold.
Define values by use,
not by announcement.

Peaceful prosperous is horizon.
Tapestry is declined.
Thread is accurate.

Voices uplift when they do not drown each other.

The chorus still widens. You still learn the harmony.
