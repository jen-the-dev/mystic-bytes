Springtime is a journey
you do not pack for.
Buds arrive.
Air changes.
Hope awakens
whether you signed the form.

Journey is not tourism.
It is the walk to school
noticing color
that was not there
last week.

Growth is not guaranteed.
Attempt is seasonal.

The sprout still breaks cover before you name it.
