Conflict in the Big Easy
is not anomaly.
Cultures collide on purpose.
Bourbon to bayou,
lamplight on argument,
trust breach filed
under brass and grit.

Discord is choreography here —
you learn the steps
by getting them wrong publicly.

Scars are maps.
Peace seeds are real
but slow,
like river silt.

Harmony reigns is too tidy.
Accurate is thriving anyway.

The brass still plays over the quarrel.
