Odyssey in New Orleans
starts before you pack.
The street is already voyage —
corner surprise,
cousin's rumor,
brass redirecting your plan.

Comfort zone is a motel here.
Wanderlust does not retire
after one circumnavigation.
It invoices the return.

Transformation is not scenic only.
It is who you become
when the city keeps sending you
back through the same door
changed.

The next corner still holds the surprise.
