Family is web, not wallpaper —
kin and lineage intertwined,
kindred harder to counterfeit
than convenience friendship.

Generations stand in weather
others only read about.
Each sphere unique;
the story still one ledger.

History told in moments dwelled —
eldest to youngest,
joy and strife both ink.

Unbreakable is a promise, not a fact.
True is what you keep
when the room demands payment.

Cherish is maintenance:
showing up when the feast
and the funeral share a calendar.

The blood still binds. You still honor the thread.
