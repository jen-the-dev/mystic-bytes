Justice is not a halo.
It is the fire you keep lit
so the harmed can find the shore
without you claiming to be the sea.

Metaphor only —
no ballot box in this stanza,
only the body learning
the difference between witness
and performance.

The beacon costs wood and lung.
The comfortable will ask you
to dim it for the aesthetic.

Keep the flame honest about fuel.

The woodpile is almost gone.
