Love is not a calm sea.
It is flirtation as weather front,
intimacy as harbor fees,
passion as engine heat
you pretend is sustainable.

Desire fuels the long nights.
So does loneliness.
Learn the difference
before you call either romance.

Pitfalls are not metaphors only.
Beautiful dreams sour.
The account does not care
what you named the voyage.

Cherish is a verb, not a refund.

The harbor fees are due at dawn.
