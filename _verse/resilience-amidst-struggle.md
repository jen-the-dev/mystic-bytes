Struggle does not issue diplomas.
Trust frays.
Wills collide.
The protagonists remain
in the same house afterward.

Resilience is not victory dressed for photos.
It is the fabric holding
one more week of wear
without pretending the seam is new.

Hope is noted as present —
not as finale.

Courage is the choice to stay accurate
when the story wants a bow.

The seam is still visible in the light.
