Friendship is not a slogan.
It is trust filed over years —
the hand extended
when romance would have been easier to explain.

Brotherhood, sisterhood:
words for alliances
that survive awkwardness
and do not keep score in public.

Enduring does not mean painless.
It means the bond
absorbs the invoice
and stays anyway.

Eternal bliss is declined.
Accurate is showing up
with the takeout
after the breakup.

The group chat still pings at midnight.
