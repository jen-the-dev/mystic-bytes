Life arrives pickled in conundrum —
crux unclear, enigma accurate,
fear not always wrong.

Puzzlement reigns while you decode
a charade that did not publish rules.
Clues sparse because you are early,
not because you are cursed.

Intervene anyway.
That is the tax on being awake.

Twists baffle. Hearts yearn.
Truth without shame is a standard, not a schedule.

The unknown may be wicked.
It may be overblown.
Riddle stays unsolved
while curiosity stays lit —
stubborn, unpretty, useful.

Dig deep.
Secrets you keep are keys you hoard.

Freedom is not guaranteed at the lock.

The puzzle still holds. You still turn what you have.
