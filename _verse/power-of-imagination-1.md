Imagination is realm, not retreat —
creation alive, senses engaged,
unseen unfolding because someone looked sideways.

Originality flows through thought
like current through wire.
Canvas is not decoration.
It is where ideas become accountable.

Boundless is a flattering lie.
Even wonder has edges —
fuel, frame, wrists that tire.

Dreams take form and invoice you.
Innovation eats imagination
and calls the meal progress.

Treasure the gift like a tool.
Rare is not harmless.
Build worlds that survive daylight
without requiring applause.

Incomparable is declined.
Honest is enough.

The canvas stays wet. You still reach in.
