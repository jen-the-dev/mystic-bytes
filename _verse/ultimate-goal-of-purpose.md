Bliss is not a permanent visa.
It is pinnacle weather —
joy beyond measure for a moment
you were present enough to enter.

Euphoria holds, then releases.
That is not betrayal.
That is physics.

Paradise sought is not wrong.
Wrong is requiring paradise
to rent the whole calendar.

Nirvana as destination is declined.
Completeness is closer —
soul unchained for an hour,
sweet sound heard without performance.

Ecstasy surpasses nothing forever.
Spell breaks. Breath returns.
You still have to wash a dish.

Purpose is not a shining brochure.
It is path followed with might
knowing light flickers
and still counts when it does.

Bright life is adventure without amnesia.

The path still runs. You still walk without requiring bliss to stay.
