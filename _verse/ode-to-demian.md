Demian — roasted without shame,
ground daily, brewed on schedule,
aroma sweet, flavor that argues back politely.

Cup of sunshine is brochure talk.
Accurate is warmth with edge.

Served with grace even out of place —
rich taste putting smile on face
is labor, not personality cult.

Heart of the cup, fuel kept upright.
Friend to all is declined.
Accurate is reliable heat.

Continue brewing. The room still depends on it.
