Faith is not proof.
It is the refusal to sell the keys
at the crossroads
when every reasonable voice
offers a better price.

Hecate does not reward certainty.
She rewards the walk back
to the threshold
after you have already lied once
about what you saw there.

Unyielding is not virtuous
when it is vanity.
It is virtuous
when it is the body
choosing one more honest step
without applause.

The keys stay in your palm.
