Truth does not wear a badge in this market.
It arrives late, underdressed,
without the confidence of a lie.

Deception has staff.
Polished decks, practiced pauses,
the handshake that means
check your pockets later.

You were taught to seek light.
Nobody mentioned the audit trail.

Follow what costs the teller something.
Follow what survives
when no audience is left to impress.

The lie closes at five.
Truth keeps odd hours
and expects you to show up anyway.

The ledger does not balance tonight.
