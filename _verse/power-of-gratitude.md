Gratitude is not a brand exercise.
It is naming what kept you alive
without pretending
what harmed you was curriculum.

Thanks for the meal.
Thanks for the friend who returned your call.
Thanks for the body
that failed you gracefully once
and may fail again.

Power is not positivity.
It is refusing to let bitterness
own the whole estate
when a room still has light.

The harm stays in the ledger.
So does the bread.
