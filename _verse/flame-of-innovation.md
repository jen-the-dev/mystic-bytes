Innovation is not novelty cosplay.
It breaches boundary —
originality with nerve,
bold enough to fail in public.

Talent without vision is noise.
See anew with precision
or repeat the room's wallpaper.

Clever helps. Courage invoices.

Break mold is not slogan.
It is splinter in the hand.

Elevate is not hierarchy fantasy.
It is craft that raises the floor
for the next maker.

Flame ignites and eats fuel.
Tend it or burn the house
and call it genius.

The mold still cracks. You still push through.
