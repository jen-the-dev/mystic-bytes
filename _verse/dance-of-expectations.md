Expectation is choreography rehearsed in private —
promise of what should arrive,
design you mistake for law.

Motives push. Prospects loom.
Hope abounds until the grip tightens
and potential narrows.

Hold the sight too hard
and you miss the sideways door —
not because you lack capacity,
because you refused the redraw.

Stone was always a lie told for comfort.
Path is pencil, not contract.

Open mind leaves margin
for the future to be larger
than the story you practiced.

Unknown is not insult.
It is the dance floor
when the planned steps fail.

Watch life begin to move
when you stop commanding the beat.

The music still shifts. You still learn the new step.
