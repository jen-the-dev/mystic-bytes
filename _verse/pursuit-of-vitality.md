Vitality is not a filter.
It is vessel condition —
body, mind, potency you feel
before the mirror comments.

Robustness is maintenance culture:
exercise, food, rest,
unphotogenic love for the organism.

Illness absent is not the whole story.
Wholeness is harmony with limits,
not domination of limits.

Nature's energy completes nothing for free.
You still do the daily labor.

At your best does not mean painless.
It means equipped for ordinary war.

Joy and delight allowed —
not required as proof.

Obstacles still arrive.
Power is continuing with a body
you kept honest.

The vessel still hums. You still tend it.
