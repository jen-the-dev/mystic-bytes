The quest is not a highway.
It is a room you re-enter
with different furniture each time.

Truth does not volunteer.
You bargain for it
with years and appetites
you are ashamed to itemize.

Some answers arrive as losses.
Some as permissions
you were not seeking.

The divine connection advertised
is often loneliness
with better stationery.

Keep the question alive
longer than the comfort of an answer.

The door stays unlatched.
