Nature in New Orleans
does not apologize for force.
Water falls,
current argues,
foam writes temporary scripture
on stone that has seen worse.

Might is not domination only.
It is resilience with sound —
roar that teaches listening,
mist that teaches pause.

Symphony is declined.
Cascade is accurate.

The falls keep speaking after you walk away.
