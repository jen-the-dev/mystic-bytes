Joy arrives without permission —
brief, bodily,
a brightness that does not negotiate
with the week's prior damage.

Bliss is not a permanent visa.
It is a window opened
long enough to prove
the dark room has air.

Elation does not dispel nightfall on contract.
It interrupts it.

The text declines eternal harmony.
It records the interruption
and the return of ordinary weather.

The window closes. The air was real.
