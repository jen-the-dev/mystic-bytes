Figure on the road — abode unknown.
Friend, foe, or weather passing through.

Guest with outstretched hand.
Migrant seeking different.
Visitor from far may spar or gift.

Acquaintance not yet made.
Story shakes categories.

Fate not yours to seal.
Brief stay or long intrigue —
both teach revision.

See anew is the toll.
Pre-approval declined.

The stranger still walks. You still revise the view.
