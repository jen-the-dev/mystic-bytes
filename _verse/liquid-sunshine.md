Liquid sunshine is what adults call rain
when they want you to be grateful.
Fair enough —
drops do shine,
grass does restart,
stress does relocate
for a minute.

Divine is declined.
Rhythmic flow is accurate.

Thirteen knows past pain
does not wash clean.
It gets quieter
while the storm speaks.

The gold light returns when the cloud moves on.
