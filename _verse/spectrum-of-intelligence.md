Intelligence is more than stored fact.
It is mind applied to flourish —
insight, perception, literacy in consequence.

Keen delves past superficial
into purpose submerged.
Brains glean so navigation
is chosen, not drifted.

Brilliance wears many uniforms —
not IQ alone,
not academic display alone.

Perceive with senses wide.
Expanse is not automatic.
It is practice.

Words are not the whole instrument.
Application stirs —
better world is trace left
after the room clears.

Nurture mind like brass:
tune, miss, repeat, listen.

Goals still cost what goals cost.

The spectrum still widens. You still learn its colors.
