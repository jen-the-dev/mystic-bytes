The bird does not lecture.
It lifts —
wing trusting air,
gust accepted,
trust not innocence,
calculation with feathers.

Freedom looks easy from the ground.
Thirteen knows better:
height is rent,
landing is negotiation,
sky is not yours,
only borrowed.

Learn is accurate.
Soar is optional.

The bird keeps moving past the lesson you wanted.
