Rhythm in New Orleans
is not background.
It is contract —
Congo Square to Preservation Hall,
Treme story,
second line demanding your feet
tell the truth.

Poets and horns share payroll.
Moonlight is not romance only.
It is overtime.

Harmonious does not mean polite.
It means many pulses
agreeing long enough
to call it a song.

The song keeps resonating after you leave the hall.
