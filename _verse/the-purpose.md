Purpose is not a PDF from heaven.
It is the argument you keep having
with your own appetites
until one appetite wins
or learns to share.

Some mornings purpose is pay rent.
Some nights it is refuse the lie
that rent is the whole theology.

The divine does not send a mission statement.
It sends consequences
and asks if you are willing
to sign without reading.

Revision is the only revelation
that repeats.

The signature dries slowly.
