The muse is not a person here.
It is petal,
line of bark,
color filed correctly
before anyone called it holy.

Divine is declined.
Intricate is accurate —
detail you notice
when you stop performing boredom.

Cherish is not poster language.
It is looking longer
than the scroll asked.

The petal still holds its detail after you look away.
