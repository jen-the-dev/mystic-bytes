Infinity is not a trophy.
It is ceiling
you cannot touch —
stars like holes in the dark,
galaxy like spill,
you like witness
with a neck ache.

Small is not insult.
Accurate is scale.

Glory is declined.
Looking longer is the task.

The night sky keeps its count after you go inside.
