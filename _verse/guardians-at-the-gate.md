Guardians are not halos.
They are hounds at the gate —
breath on the threshold,
teeth reserved for what follows you out.

The light you wanted
was a nightlight.
What arrived was witness:
someone seeing you attempt the door
without opening it for you.

Protection is not erasure of risk.
It is company
while you learn the lock.

The dog does not explain the myth.
It waits.

The gate stays latched from the inside.
