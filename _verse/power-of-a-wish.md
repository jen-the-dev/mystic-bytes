A wish is want
given a costume of innocence.

You blow out the candle
and the smoke writes
what you are afraid to invoice.

Magic is not mercy.
It is appetite
with better lighting.

Hecate keeps the wishes
that were honest about the price.

Some come true as warnings.

The smoke still spells your name.
