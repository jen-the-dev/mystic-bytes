Wanderlust whispers
before you have miles —
map on the wall,
name you cannot pronounce,
road that might not exist
except in the book.

Travel is not distance only.
It is thought with luggage.

The whisper returns
every time the ordinary room
gets too small.

The road still calls after you close the atlas.
