Wrath is not anger like a person's.
It is wind with paperwork —
trees submitting,
rain sheeting,
thunder correcting the sky's volume.

No safety is not drama.
It is math:
you are smaller,
the storm is older,
the house is a bet.

Thirteen learns the difference
between scared
and impressed.

The gales pass. The trees remember how to stand crooked.
