Pet is companion with receipts —
dog energy, cat independence,
turtle armor, rabbit nose,
snake calm, parrot song when blue.

Species differ. Contract similar:
comfort, habit, care returned in species accent.

Hold tight is not ownership only.
It is maintenance through ugly weeks.

Cherish each one.
More than animal —
roommate who does not read your excuses.

Delight is daily, not montage.

The leash still pulls. You still show up.
