Rain in New Orleans
is not background noise.
It drums the cobblestones,
rinses the oak,
files worry
into the gutter
with the same persistence
as a relative who means well.

Symphony is declined.
Percussion is accurate —
each drop a vote
for beginning again,
whether you requested it.

Liquid sunshine is brochure copy.
Accurate is soak:
body reminded
the city also weeps.

The patter keeps time after the clouds move on.
