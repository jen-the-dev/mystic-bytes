The mountain does not care about your triumph song.
It offers thin air,
steep test,
valley small only if you earned the height.

Each step is argument:
grit against gravity,
lungs against pride.

The view is payment,
not gift.

Thirteen is old enough
to love the summit
and know the descent
is also coming.

The valley waits. It was always there.
