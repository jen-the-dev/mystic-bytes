A glance is not a contract.
It is a door opened one inch —
peek into the soul,
or peek into the appetite;
often the same hallway.

Brief is the honest word.
The body ticks forward
before language arrives
to launder the want.

Volumes without words
are usually invoices unpaid.

Do not build a life
on a blink.
Build it, if you build,
on what happens
after the blink refuses to close.

The door is still open one inch.
