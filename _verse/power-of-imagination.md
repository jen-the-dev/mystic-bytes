Imagination is not a vacation.
It is forge and field —
extraordinary lit because someone looked sideways,
senses engaged, bill still due.

Unseen waits like lumber in the yard.
Venture of mind becomes reality
only when hands show up.

Originality through thought
is not purity.
It is appetite disciplined
until it makes form.

Dreams consequential means they invoice you.
Innovation eats wonder
and calls the meal progress.

No bounds is brochure talk.
Even wonder has edges —
fuel, frame, wrists that tire.

Treasure like a tool.
Build rare that survives daylight.

The forge still heats. You still hammer the unseen.
