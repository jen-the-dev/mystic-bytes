Confidence is not volume.
It is posture
with receipts —
stand tall when earned,
soften when brash
starts sounding like fear
in a louder hat.

Self-esteem is maintenance.
Doubt arrives anyway.
The art is not erasing it.
The art is walking
while it comments.

Inner voice is not oracle.
It is roommate.
Trust is negotiated daily.

Come alive is declined.
Accurate is continue
with poise that can sit down.

The doubt still knocks. You still answer the door.
