Summer assigns jurisdiction:
sun owns the skin,
sand owns the shoes,
water owns the knees.

Serenity is not ceasefire.
It is pause —
castle built,
wave erased,
body learning salt.

Oasis is accurate.
Permanent is declined.

The tide still rewrites the castle after you leave.
