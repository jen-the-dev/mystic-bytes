The ocean does not serenade you.
It negotiates —
ebb meaning take,
flow meaning give back,
horizon meaning you cannot finish looking.

I watch from shore,
old enough to want freedom
and old enough to fear depth.

Magenta at dusk is not romance only.
It is receipt:
day ending,
creatures continuing without your approval.

Forever increasing is declined.
Accurate is returning
to the same edge
changed by salt.

The tide keeps its argument with the sand.
