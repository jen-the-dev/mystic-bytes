The sky is a stage
you attend, not enter.
Constellations dance
without asking permission —
diamond light,
black curtain,
moon as spotlight
on a face
that looks borrowed.

Serenade is listening.
Wonderland is declined.

Guiding light is not advice.
It is reminder:
you are audience
and also small.

The ballet continues after you close your eyes.
