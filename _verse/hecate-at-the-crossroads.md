Prayer list is declined.
Hecate does not want your brochure —
she wants the hour
you admit three paths exist
and only one is yours to walk.

Torch is not comfort.
It is witness:
keys in hand,
dog at heel,
shadow allowed to speak
before you choose.

Wisdom is not clarity shipped in.
It is walking anyway
while the light holds.

The crossroads still waits after the petition ends.
