Quickened steps are not panic
when the stride is chosen.

Momentum accrues quietly —
not montage,
Tuesday after Tuesday.

Battle is too grand a word
for the ordinary courage
of continuing while afraid.

Caution and determination share a room.
Fresh start is not amnesia.
It is new inventory.

Fear comments. It does not get the veto
unless you hand it the pen.

Stress left behind still leaves footprints.
You learn the path by walking
with purpose that can sit down.

Discovery is not a bright tomorrow delivered.
It is the map you draw
while wrath, weather, and welcome
argue over the same street.

The stride still quickens. You still gather strength inside.
