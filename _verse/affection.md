Affection begins as itch —
crush, spark, the body voting
before the mind files paperwork.

Amour is not abstract.
It is daily:
touch that means stay,
trust that means return,
weather that means stay anyway.

Divine shine is declined.
Accurate is tingle
on a Tuesday,
not a billboard.

Glue is unglamorous.
So is survival.

The bond sets another layer overnight.
