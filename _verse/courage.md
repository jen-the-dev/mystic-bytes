Courage is not winning every fight.
It is remaining in the room
when leaving would earn applause
from the part of the self
that prefers neat endings.

Dauntless is a marketing word.
What endures is smaller:
the refusal to perform bravery
while still taking the next step.

Heroes are for other people's stories.
This account records
the ordinary nerve
required to face a Tuesday
after Monday broke something important.

The Tuesday arrives anyway.
