Eternity is too large to carry.
Existence fits in seconds —
this one,
maybe the next,
none promised.

Immortality is a daydream.
Mortality is the clock
on the classroom wall.

Legacy is not poster language.
Accurate is memory
you bother to make
before the bell rings.

The second hand still counts whether you are ready.
