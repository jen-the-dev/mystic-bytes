Joy in New Orleans
echoes before it explains itself —
smile on the corner,
laughter in the bakery line,
music leaking from a door
you were not invited through
and still benefit from.

A grin here is not decoration.
It is bridge currency —
short distance,
high value,
spent fast.

Tapestry is declined.
Accurate is overlap:
your happiness
briefly belongs
to the block.

The echo returns on the next corner.
