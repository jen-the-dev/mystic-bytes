Eternity in a moment
is breath held too long —
sun on the arm,
distant hum,
senses voting present
before the mind
schedules the next thing.

Savor is not trap.
Accurate is notice.

Forever sustains is half true.
Memory sustains is accurate.

The breath releases. The moment keeps its copy.
