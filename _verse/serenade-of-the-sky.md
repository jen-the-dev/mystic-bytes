Clouds over New Orleans
do not rush their set.
Cotton, thunderhead, silver dissolving —
each teaches drift.

Serenade is not romance only.
It is weather with patience,
sky filing mood
above a city
that refuses stillness.

Eternal youth is declined.
Accurate is watching
something change shape
while you remain
temporarily lighter.

The canvas shifts. You keep looking up.
