A kiss is not a brand promise.
It is appetite with a shorter fuse —
lips negotiating territory
the mind has not authorized.

Tender can be accurate.
So can hungry.
The body keeps both receipts.

Connection deepens when the mouth
stops performing innocence
and admits what it came for.

Surrender is not always virtue.
Sometimes it is the pulse
choosing the wrong room
and staying anyway.

The lipstick smears on the cup rim.
