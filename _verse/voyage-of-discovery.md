Embarking is not costume.
It is vessel —
real or imagined —
crossing sea, land, sky
because why will not sit still.

Waves roar is honest.
Pressing on is accurate.

Treasure is not always object.
Sometimes it is sensation
you did not pack for.

The horizon still holds what you have not uncovered.
