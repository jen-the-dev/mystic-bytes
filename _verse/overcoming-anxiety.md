The body keeps its own calendar.
Pulse elevated without permission.
Mind rehearsing catastrophes
it will not invoice you for.

This is not a character flaw report.
It is physiology wearing a narrative.

Breath can be counted —
not as victory,
as temporary treaty
with the animal that lives
behind the ribs.

Speech with a witness helps
when the witness does not rush you
toward a moral ending.

Anxiety does not require redemption.
It requires room
and the refusal to call fear a failure.

The hands stay in view.
That is the whole assignment.
