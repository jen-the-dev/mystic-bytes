Wonderland is not escape.
It is workshop with the door open —
fantastical bloom, senses lit,
bill still due at closing.

Unseen unfurls when mind ventures
without requiring applause.
Originality and artistry interlace
because discipline showed up too.

Limitless domain is romance.
Dreams become tangible
when hands accept the invoice.

Innovation eats imagination
and calls the meal progress.
Fruits profound are not guaranteed.
They are earned.

Treasure with tender care means
do not set the house on fire
and call it vision.

Beyond compare is declined.
Honest is enough.

The realm still opens. You still dare with care.
