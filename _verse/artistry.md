Artistry in New Orleans
ignores the mold.
Faubourg Marigny color,
Garden District refusal,
river as moving brief —
each says make anyway.

Symphony is declined.
Surprise is the method:
jazz phrase that detours,
stroke that admits the hand,
dream that costs rent.

Unplanned is not accident only.
It is the city teaching
who you are
when the outline fails.

The canvas still wet after midnight.
