Intelligence is not trivia hoarded.
It is how the mind grows into use —
insight, perception, literacy in consequence.

Shrewd sees past surface
into purpose submerged.
Brains discern and learn
so the turn is chosen, not drifted into.

Brilliance is not one test score.
It is sense applied —
world read through more than one window.

What you say matters less
than what you do with what you know.
Better place is not a slogan.
It is trace left after the room clears.

Cultivate the mind like an instrument:
tune, practice, miss, repeat.

Anything you set out to do
still costs what anything costs.

The mind still sharpens. You still apply the edge.
