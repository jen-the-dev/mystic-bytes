The bag lifted before dawn —
not profit,
the ritual of a small theft
while grind and water
still pretended marriage.

Beans scattered: evidence
of careless want.
Kneeling,
each bean named
a reason to return to bed
and none taken.

The grinder knew.
It always knows —
small metal verdict,
humming while the thief
insisted caffeine,
not the kitchen
that wasn't theirs yet,
not the pulse
arguing with dark.

Empires rebuild in cups.
This is not empire.
Only minutes stolen
from whoever they're owed —
including the self
that keeps pouring.

You still pour another.
