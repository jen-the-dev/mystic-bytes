Forward is a verb with splinters.
Hastened steps still need a floor.

Strength is mustered, not delivered —
drawn up from the place
stress tried to lease.

Momentum builds the way humidity does:
felt before named,
heavy, then ordinary.

Progress can be slow
and still count.
The fray is not always noble.
Sometimes it is Tuesday.

Caution with heart beats swagger with debt.
Fresh start is inventory, not erasure.

Thick and thin are both weather.
Doubt comments.
You keep walking.

Best is not a peak you summit once.
It is the version of you
that stops dragging the old unrest
like a suitcase you forgot to open.

Purpose with grace means you can sit down
and still belong to the direction.

The steps still forward. You still choose the pace.
