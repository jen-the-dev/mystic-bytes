Joy in New Orleans
is not a vacation.
It is statehood —
French Quarter pulse,
Mississippi shoulder,
horn section teaching the chest
that sorrow and elation
share rent.

Timeless melody is brochure copy.
Accurate is the tune
you hum while doing dishes,
while grieving,
while waiting for the streetcar.

No limit is false.
Joy here knows closing time.
It returns anyway.

The horn finds the key again.
