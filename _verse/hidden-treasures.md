Treasure is not always gold.
Sometimes it is drawer,
box under the bed,
note you were not meant to read yet,
truth that glitters
only after you ask.

Hidden is invitation.
Wealth is declined as moral.

Bold is accurate:
look anyway,
pay the consequence,
keep the story.

The trove still holds one door you have not opened.
