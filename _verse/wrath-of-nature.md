Nature's wrath is not personal.
It does not hate your house.
It passes through —
wind, rain, light,
the land remembering
it was here first.

Bound is accurate:
not chained for drama,
just sized correctly
against the roar.

Humanity is the guest.
The storm is the host.

The surround loosens. The accounting remains.
