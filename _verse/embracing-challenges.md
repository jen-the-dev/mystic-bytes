Challenges in New Orleans
are not motivational posters.
They are Mississippi current —
twist, undertow,
the block still expecting dinner.

Resilience is not anthem.
It is daily receipt:
gumption when glamour fails,
conviction when the road
floods mid-argument.

Triumph is declined.
Accurate is continuing
under skies that already saw worse.

The current still pulls. You still cross.
