Some kindnesses are too specific
for a public signal —
rubber chicken on the porch,
terrier trouble,
cocker spaniel grace
that refuses to file grievance.

Winston and Chewy stay off the shelf:
rewritten, kept,
names that belong to memory,
not the archive.

What the porch taught you
is yours to carry.
