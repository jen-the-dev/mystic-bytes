Friendship is a reflection
you cannot always flattering-angle.

Trust is built in boring hours —
rides to the airport,
truth told when kindness would lie,
silence that is not punishment.

Brotherhood without performance.
Sisterhood without audition.

Lifetime defined is too tidy.
Accurate is the thread
still holding after the year
you were not lovable.

The helping hand is not charity.
It is reciprocity
with memory attached.

The thread still holds one frayed edge.
