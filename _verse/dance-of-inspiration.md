Inspiration in New Orleans
does not wait for permission.
It arrives with Spanish moss,
street rumor,
jazz turning the corner
before you have a subject.

Spark is cheap.
Accurate is follow-through —
brushstroke, set list,
gumbo stirred while the idea
still burns.

The Mississippi is not metaphor only.
It is deadline:
keep moving,
keep gleaming,
or sink into your own delay.

The muse keeps playing past midnight.
