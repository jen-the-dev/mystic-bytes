An embrace is a treaty of minutes.
Eternal is what people say
when the minutes feel expensive enough.

Passion burns on contact.
Desire sustains the lonely hours.
Both can be true
without promising safety.

Shadows in love are not plot twists.
They are the room's other furniture.

Hold tightly.
Release honestly.
Do not call either virtue
without reading the receipt.

The arms unlock. The receipt remains.
