Foresight in New Orleans
is not a vision board.
It is hands on the next rebuild —
promise kept
when the horizon
looks like rumor.

Tapestry is labor.
Dreams become land
by walking them
through humidity,
bureaucracy,
and brass.

Impossible is declined.
Accurate is trying anyway
with witnesses.

The next block still waits for your hands.
