A day in the sun is not a novel.
It is dew on grass,
bird noise,
wind in leaves,
heat climbing until
the body files a complaint
and calls it joy anyway.

Radiant is accurate.
Strife-free is not.

Thirteen learns to bask
and seek shade
in the same hour.

Delightful is the shade arriving on time.
