The wanderer arrives
without filing a form —
New Orleans knows this face:
story in the coat,
purpose in the pocket,
maybe friend,
maybe question.

Enigma is not threat only.
It is invitation:
grow a wider horizon,
pay attention,
do not pretend
you already finished the map.

Forever unsolved is possible.
Still changed is likely.

The stranger's step still echoes after they turn the corner.
