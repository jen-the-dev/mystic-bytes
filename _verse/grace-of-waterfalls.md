Waterfall grace is not gentle.
It is descent with opinion —
height turned into noise,
rock taught a new shape,
mist on the face
like unsolicited baptism.

Possibility is real.
So is force.

I stand far enough to admire,
close enough to feel small —
thirteen's favorite geography.

Cherish is accurate.
Romanticize is declined.

The pool still receives what the cliff releases.
