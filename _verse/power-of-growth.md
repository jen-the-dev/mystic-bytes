Growth is surge and invoice —
prosperity whispered, branches wide,
sprout reaching without permission.

Each day adds strength you notice
only when old fear finds the door locked.

Obstacles boost will or bruise it.
Thrill fuels until it doesn't.
Bloom waits in the bud —
no command performance.

Horizons move when walked.
Limits pushed teach spine.
Spirit built in rooms
no one photographed.

Never-ending is accurate.
Hope unbending is declined.
Hope working is closer.

Self-discovery is shed skin, not brand.
Transformation keeps possibilities as key —
not promise, tool.

Inch closer to light without requiring light
to rent the whole calendar.

The seed still splits. Growth still charges the ground.
