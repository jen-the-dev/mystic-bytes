Heart and soul travel
before the body gets a ticket —
border in the mind,
mountain in the book,
coast in the song,
secret not named yet.

Miles are proof.
Smiles are receipt.

Explore never grows old
because the self
keeps outgrowing rooms.

The road still asks one question you have not answered.
