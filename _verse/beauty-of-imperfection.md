Nature signs its work with flaw —
foible visible, uniqueness not negotiable.

Blemish mars the billboard.
It deepens the portrait you live in.

Imperfection owned is human proof:
flesh, bone, error,
not a defect in the assembly line.

Shortcoming impresses
when struggle is included in the résumé.

Do not lament scars
you earned honestly.
Some bars are mercy.
Some are map.

Connection finds the fault line.
Quirks are coordinates, not debris.

Perfectly imperfect is consent
to be readable without audition.

Nature did not promise symmetry.
It promised signature.

The scar still shows. You still wear it as yours.
