Humility is overlooked because it refuses theater.
Reserve does not boast.
Bashfulness is not defect —
it is volume control on the self.

Meek is not erased.
It is lowly by choice,
not by verdict.

Modesty shines without competing.
Contrition repairs what pride cracked —
amends as craft, not performance.

Gentle withstands time
because it does not spend itself proving.

Truly rich spirit survives setbacks
without rebranding them as war.

Embody in every endeavor
means daily, unphotogenic, repeated.

Peace and serenity are not prizes.
They are what remains
when ego stops renting the whole house.

The quiet still carries. You still choose low.
