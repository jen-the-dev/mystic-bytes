Ambition is appetite in professional dress.
Faith is appetite that learned to wait
without pretending waiting is virtue.

Optimism is the third roommate —
loud, helpful,
sometimes wrong about the rent.

Empowering is not cheerleading.
It is choosing the next honest step
when the step does not photograph well.

Hecate charges for clarity.
Ambition wants a discount.
Faith pays in installments.

The kettle boils in the shared kitchen.
