Ashes do not promise wings.
They record what burned.

The heartbroken night
is not a training montage.
It is a quiet house
and a cup that will not be filled.

Resurgence, if it arrives,
does not erase the ash.
It walks through it
and learns the weight of the shoes.

Stronger is a word strangers offer.
The text prefers accurate:
still here,
still breathing,
still unable to tidy the ending.

The ash stays on the threshold.
