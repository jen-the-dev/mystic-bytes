In the realm of cards fantasy thrives —
Butchers arrive with blade and appetite.

Horde butcher: chaos commanded,
feast on strength, land reordered.

Malakir butcher: crimson stage,
life drained prey by prey.

Bridge butcher claims spirits one by one
beneath setting sun.

Abyssal persecutor — dread hand,
darkness where they stand.

Ghoul butcher feasts, rises again,
relentless decree.

Collector friend, these pieces you adore —
essence gleams in battle and lore.

Arcane dig is not escape.
It is inventory of appetite
wearing cardboard skin.

The blade still gleams. You still sort the deck.
