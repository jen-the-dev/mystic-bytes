Sadness arrives as tidal math —
hope displaced,
light under water,
the chest learning new altitudes.

Grief and sorrow walk beside the body
like witnesses who will not leave
when the room asks them to.

Despair is not a personality.
It is exhaustion
with better vocabulary.

The text refuses sweet oblivion
as a happy ending.
If a glimmer exists,
it is noted without parade:
night continuing,
breath continuing,
the flood not finished.

The waterline is still marked on the door.
