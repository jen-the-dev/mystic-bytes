Embrace is a body word.
You do not embrace an argument.
You embrace the weight
that will not leave when logic does.

Faith's power is not certainty.
It is showing up at the crossing
again after the first answer
humiliated you.

Hecate does not ask for belief.
She asks for presence
without rescue fantasy.

The embrace tightens once, then loosens.
That is the whole lesson.

The weight remains. So do you.
