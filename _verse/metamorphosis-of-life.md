Life revises without permission —
knowledge shifts, view swapped,
old sway retired without ceremony.

Metamorphosis is not serene by default.
It is shed skin on the floor
while you still answer to the old name.

Transmutation sounds holy.
Mostly it is labor at ebb and flow —
give, take, react,
track laid by reaction.

Variance opens receipts.
Belief is what you build
after the beautiful dream stops returning calls.

Daunting is accurate.
Embrace does not mean approve.
It means stay in the room
while the furniture changes.

Bigger scheme is optional theology.
Closer truth: you outgrew a version
you were still performing.

The skin still splits. Life still enters the next room.
