Endless is the point.
Travel is not odometer only —
border crossed,
mountain seen,
smile in a language
you do not own yet,
thought you could not have
at home.

Ordinary left behind
does not mean ordinary was wrong.
It means room was needed.

Exploration does not retire.
It waits for the next ticket.

The road still offers one place you have not named.
