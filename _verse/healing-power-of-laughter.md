Laughter is sound of joy with teeth —
medicine that does not refund grief,
belly release, shriek of delight,
howl at joke, wolf at moon optional.

Rejoicing, merriment, friends at party:
life audible when the room stops performing.

Best medicine is not replacement.
It is brief treaty with the body.

Laugh when you can.
Fill with joy where joy is honest.

Hear it daily if possible.
Not required to solve the ailment.

The laugh still bursts. You still let it land.
