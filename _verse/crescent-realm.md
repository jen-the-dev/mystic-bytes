The call is not distant.
It is New Orleans
asking you to leave comfort
without leaving the parish —
uncharted block,
roof you were told not to climb,
river view that costs nerve.

Realm is not fantasy.
It is jurisdiction
where the dauntless get named
by what they attempt,
not what they post.

Leap is optional.
Showing up is not.

The block still holds the uncharted door.
