The unseen is not always sacred.
Sometimes it is the debt
you have not opened.

Embrace does not mean invite forever.
It means stop pretending
the room is empty
when the air changes thickness
as you enter.

What you cannot see
still negotiates with your pulse.

Hecate's gift is not vision.
It is refusal to look away
from the corner
where your optimism died politely.

The air changes thickness again.
