Journey, pilgrimage, sojourn —
names for the same tax on comfort:
roam, traverse, seek,
answers withheld on schedule.

Odyssey and expedition romance the unknown.
Circumnavigation complete is one story.
Wanderlust impossible is also one story.

Destination matters less than revision —
who you become while miles argue with your habits.

Lessons won are not trophies.
They are reflex you did not have at departure.

Every step is gift only if inhabited.
New dawn is not amnesia.

Transformation is not A to B.
It is the person who can read the map afterward
without requiring the map to be pretty.

Seize opportunities without requiring them to stay.
Cherish because they leave.

The voyage defines you. You still travel with the revision visible.
