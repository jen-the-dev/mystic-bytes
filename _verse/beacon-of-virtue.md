A beacon is not purity.
It is a fire you maintain
so others can navigate
without asking who pays the wood.

Virtue advertised is virtue exhausted.
The beacon works
only if you accept
the smoke in your own lungs.

Signal without warmth is lighthouse cruelty —
visible, unmoved, proud of the distance.

Better a small lamp honest about fuel
than a skyline performance of goodness.

The woodpile is lower than yesterday.
