Maturity is voyage, not harbor —
childhood traded for judgment,
experience stacked like rings
you only count later.

Sophistication follows consequence.
Wisdom is error translated into reflex.

Wise and adept means learning
without hiring compromise as virtue.

Foundation is nights no one photographed.
Use knowledge well —
cherish moment without requiring moment
to save you.

Embrace with open heart
does not mean open ledger.
Best is alignment —
body, nerve, work claimed.

Happiness is not constant sun.
It is direction kept
while weather argues.

The voyage continues. You still sail by what ripened you.
