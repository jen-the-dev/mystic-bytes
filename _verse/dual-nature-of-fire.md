Fire keeps two accounts.
In one ledger: hearth, amber, the hands held out.
In the other: wildline, smoke, what the map will not recover.

The same element cooks the meal
and writes the evacuation order.

Respect is not fear worship.
It is knowing which room the flame is auditing
before you call it comfort.

A spark does not negotiate.
The body learns the difference
between warmth and warning.

The smoke alarm still remembers both.
