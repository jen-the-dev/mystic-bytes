Conflict frays the fabric
faster than the repair kit arrives.
Trust breaches.
Wills collide.
The room becomes a contest
no one remembers agreeing to enter.

Struggle is not a seminar.
It is the day after the argument
when both people are still there
and the problem is not solved.

Victory is removed from the title.
What remains is endurance:
learning what the fight cost,
whether the lesson was worth the invoice.

Hope persists as fact, not finale.

The fabric is still frayed at the seam.
