Weakness invokes indictment —
frailty, folly, vice on display,
might absent, limits accurate.

Shortcoming is data pride misfiles.
Vulnerability is hard to bear
because exposure is true.

Still weakness teaches reach:
flaw and imperfection
as connection's door, not exile.

Humility lives in limits named.
Strength in dimness is not glow.
It is continuing while lit low.

Embrace does not mean celebrate collapse.
It means stop performing invincible
long enough to learn the address of help.

Blessing at the core is not comfort pasted.
It is transformation with receipts.

The flaw still shows. You still step through it.
