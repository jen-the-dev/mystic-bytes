Adventure calls
the way the stairs call
when the attic light is new —
not safe,
not promised,
just there.

Leap is too grand a word.
Accurate is step:
one unknown floorboard,
one map drawn wrong,
one yes before the mind files no.

The chase keeps humming after you come home.
