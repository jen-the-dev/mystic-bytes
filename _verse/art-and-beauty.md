Art is not applause only.
It is brushstroke
arresting a moment
that was already leaving —
hue arguing with hue,
texture telling truth
the mouth has not earned.

Divine love is declined.
Accurate is looking
until the heart
files a copy.

Masterpiece is rare.
Noticing is daily.

The paint still wet after you step back.
