Fire is friendly at the hearth
and honest in the wild —
same orange,
different invoice.

Crackling comfort is real.
So is the line
where flare becomes appetite
for everything you forgot to tend.

Thirteen is old enough
to respect the warmth
and fear the spread.

Beauty shared is controlled flame.
Everything else is weather with teeth.

The ember still decides if you meant it.
