Nature in New Orleans
sings under the noise.
Magnolia, mossed oak,
Bayou St. John edge,
cicada and rain on cobble —
counter-melody to traffic.

Symphony is declined.
Chorus is accurate:
many small voices
agreeing you should pause.

Sanctuary is not escape only.
It is attention training —
sunset on the Mississippi,
wildflower on the levee,
proof the city breathes
outside your agenda.

The cicada keeps time after the horn stops.
