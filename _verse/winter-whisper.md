Snow whispers the way weather whispers —
not secret wisdom,
just quiet imposed
on a world that usually argues.

Each flake is art
you cannot keep.
Drifts against the wall
are geometry with deadline.

Stocking up is not romance.
It is respect.

Thirteen learns stillness
can feel like relief
and warning
in the same breath.

The hush lifts when the thaw begins.
