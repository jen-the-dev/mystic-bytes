Scent does not ask before it enters.
Honeysuckle on the walk home,
wet earth after rain,
coffee from a window you do not own —
each files a memory
without permission.

Thirteen learns the body keeps receipts
in the nose.
Romance is not required.
Recognition is.

Symphony is declined.
Journey is accurate:
one inhale,
whole afternoon relocated.

The air still carries what you forgot you knew.
