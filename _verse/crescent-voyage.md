Voyage in New Orleans
is not a stroll.
It is promise kept wet —
sea, land, sky,
Crescent curve,
challenge claimed,
wonder filed
with the roar still in your ears.

Feather soaring is declined.
Heart open is accurate.

Mystery expands
when you stop pretending
you already know the block.

The vessel still holds one uncharted door.
