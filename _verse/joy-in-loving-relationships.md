Winter makes the body honest.
You reach for a hand
not because romance requires it
but because heat is a bill
the skin keeps sending.

Tenderness is repair work.
Touch unlocks what talking
circled for hours.

Overflow is a pretty word.
Accurate is choosing warmth
when pride would leave you cold.

New start is not guaranteed.
Joy in relationship is smaller:
the fire still catching
after the argument,
two people still in the room.

The hand stays within reach.
