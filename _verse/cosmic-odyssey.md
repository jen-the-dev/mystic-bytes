Odyssey is not vacation.
It is years of asking —
wormhole as shortcut,
black hole as warning,
alien as mirror,
fear allowed to ride shotgun.

United stand is declined as slogan.
Accurate is continue
while the unknown
still has teeth.

Imagination soars is brochure copy.
Accurate is you still reading
when the light should be out.

The ship still travels after you close the chapter.
