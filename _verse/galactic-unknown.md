Galactic unknown is not empty.
It is full of addresses
you have not visited —
star, robot, invasion dream,
fear and wonder
sharing a seat belt.

Boldly go is permission,
not guarantee.

Secure the world is adult work.
Imagine it is child's work.
Both belong in the same sky.

The unknown keeps one star unpunctured for you.

JV · Dark Heart Labs
