Fire in New Orleans
is not always disaster.
Sometimes it is chicory
and a room
where the trumpet
makes the body
forget its landlord.

Passion here runs on ancestry —
Congo Square in the blood,
French Quarter in the laugh,
the kind of want
that does not ask permission nicely.

Keep it is too simple.
Accurate is tend —
knowing the blaze
also cooks
and also scars.

The cup stays hot after the band leaves.
