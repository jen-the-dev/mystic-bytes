Spring in New Orleans
is not subtle.
Buds enter like brass —
yellow, blue, primrose argument
against winter's receipt.

Symphony is declined.
Renewal is logistics:
scent in the air,
color on the canvas,
body reminded
the cycle still votes yes.

Bloom declares potential.
Potential still owes follow-through.

The petal opens before you name the hope.
