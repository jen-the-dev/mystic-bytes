Chance and choice share the floor.
Luck arrives, leaves, returns with opinions —
random twist that rewrites the address
you thought was permanent.

You gamble the hopeful purse
for providence's glimpse.
Double-edged is accurate:
blessing, curse, adored, abhorred.

Coin and dice change temperature fast.
Do not marry the flip.

Work is the other partner —
bright future built slow,
strength that survives bad deals.

Steadfast heart knows the ratio:
not chance alone,
not choice alone,
the dance between.

The music still changes. You still choose the step.
