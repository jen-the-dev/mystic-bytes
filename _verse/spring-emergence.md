Spring is not a concert for you.
It is evidence —
bud breaking cover,
wildflower yellow and blue,
air changed enough
to notice on the walk to school.

Symphony is declined.
Renewal is logistics:
scent,
color,
the world voting yes
before you have a plan.

Perennial is accurate.
So is temporary bloom.

The primrose still opens without your permission.
