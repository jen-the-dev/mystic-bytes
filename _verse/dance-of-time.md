Time in New Orleans
does not march.
It syncopates —
epoch and second
sharing a cigarette,
memory and now
trading the solo.

Enigma is accurate.
You cannot stem it.
You can attend.

Each tick is impact or drift.
The city teaches both.

Divine melody is declined.
Brass metronome is real:
parade starts late,
ends later,
still counts.

The second hand waits for no one. The band starts anyway.
