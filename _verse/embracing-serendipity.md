Serendipity is the street you did not plan
that still gets you fed —
happenstance with good timing,
kismet wearing work boots.

Chance introduces the fluke.
Choice decides whether the fluke becomes story
or anecdote you never repeat.

A wrong turn in New Orleans
can land you at the right porch
if you stay long enough to listen.

Do not worship the accident.
Thank it, then do the labor
it opened room for.

Cosmic flow is a pretty phrase
for attention meeting opportunity
while pride is on pause.

Surrender is not passivity.
It is staying awake
when the plot detours.

Your hands still steer.
Luck still deals the corner.
