Some people bring the lamp.
Some people are the lamp
and pretend they are not burning.

Tribute is not pedestal.
It is naming the hands
that made your week survivable —
the text at midnight,
the ride, the laugh
that reset your lungs.

Life shines because someone paid fuel.
Thank them without turning them
into your permanent sun.

Lamps overheat.
Let them rest.

The wick is shorter tonight.
