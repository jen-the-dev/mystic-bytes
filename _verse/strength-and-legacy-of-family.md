Family is web enjoyed and owed —
clan intertwined,
kindred that survives convenience.

Generations stand in shared weather.
Each sphere unique; ledger one.

History told in moments dwelled.
Unbreakable is promise maintained.

Ties bind in joy and strife.
Cherish is showing up
when the calendar turns ugly.

Legacy is not display.
It is thread you keep from fraying.

The story still continues. You still hold the thread.
