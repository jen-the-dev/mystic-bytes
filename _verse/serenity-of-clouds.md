Serenity is not stillness forever.
Clouds prove it —
cotton becoming thunderhead,
silver erased by sun,
curtain descending,
curtain lifted.

Thirteen learns peace
as attention practice:
watch the shape change,
do not own it.

Eternal truth is declined.
Accurate is refresh —
brush of calm
that does not stay brushed.

The next cloud edits the sky.
