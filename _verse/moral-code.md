A moral code is not jewelry.
It is the line you draw
before appetite offers better furniture.

Importance is tested at 2 a.m.
when the shortcut knocks
and you are tired enough
to call it wisdom.

Codes fail loudly or quietly —
the loud failure is spectacle;
the quiet one is habit.

Keep the rule you can repeat
without an audience.

The knock comes again at 2 a.m.
