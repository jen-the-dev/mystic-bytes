Affection is not a contract with forever.
It is the body choosing return —
touch remembered,
kiss invoiced,
the pulse filing the same name again.

Unwavering is a promise lovers make
before the argument arrives.
What endures longer is pattern:
show up,
want honestly,
do not call hunger virtue.

Rapture is not a permanent address.
It is a room you rent with your mouth.

The name is filed again tonight.
