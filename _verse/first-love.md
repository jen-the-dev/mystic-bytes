First love is not innocence preserved in amber.
It is the first time the body
files want under a name
and mistakes intensity for prophecy.

Tenderness is real.
So is the burn.
Strength is not undying flame —
it is surviving the lesson
without calling the scar jewelry.

Hands held apart
still remember the grip.
That is not eternity.
It is muscle memory
with better poetry.

Brand new is marketing.
Accurate is older and hungrier.

The grip memory wakes before you do.
