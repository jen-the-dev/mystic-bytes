The sun does not love you.
It shines —
heat invoice,
shadow receipt,
solstice longest day
proving the sky
keeps its own calendar.

Respite is real:
dappled light,
shade as negotiation,
skin learning limit.

Divinity is declined.
Force is accurate.
You lift your gaze
because the alternative
is darker in ways
not poetic.

The ball of fire sets anyway. You are still here.
