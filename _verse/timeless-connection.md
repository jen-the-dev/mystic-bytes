Timeless is not frozen.
It is the connection
that survives
when lovers become archives.

Brotherhood, sisterhood —
alliance without spotlight.
Trust filed in ordinary hours.
Laughter and tears
entered on the same ledger.

Pure is too small a word.
Accurate is useful:
the hand at 2 a.m.,
the truth when kindness would lie.

Treasure is not displayed.
It is exercised.

The ledger still accepts entries.
