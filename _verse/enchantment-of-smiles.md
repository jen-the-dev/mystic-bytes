A smile is not a promise.
It is a brief treaty —
lips negotiating truce,
eyes sparking yes
before language launders the want.

Enchantment is cheap.
That does not mean it is false.
It means do not build a mortgage on it.

Blissful beam is brochure copy.
Accurate is the face
opening for one second
and changing the temperature of the room.

Divine is declined.
Human is enough.

The temperature change fades. It was real.
