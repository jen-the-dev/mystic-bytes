Brotherhood is bond beyond skin —
comradeship with receipts,
solidarity that answers ugly hours.

Thick and thin share calendar.
Confidant needed because fear lies.

Side by side is logistics.
Hope guides; fear comments.

Torch in heart is duty tended —
flame eternity declines.
Accurate is keep lit for the next walker.

Strength beyond length means load-bearing,
not decorative.

Cherish grand bond by maintenance,
not announcement.

Ascend is declined.
Stand until standing teaches.

The bond still holds. You still answer the call.
