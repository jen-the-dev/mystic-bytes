Custom passes annual as tool, not wallpaper —
legend, lore, fable retold
generation to generation with edits included.

Fabric of past woven with care.
Tapestry is declined.
Thread is accurate.

Values steeped deep in mind.
Legacy held, not sold.

Ancestors alive in practice —
beliefs that thrive when used,
not embalmed.

Connection in soul to old beckoning:
tradition is who you are
because you keep the verb.

Heritage dear is maintenance.

The thread still runs. You still weave it forward.
