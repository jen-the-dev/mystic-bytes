Unfairness shows its face —
dread accurate when the scale
was rigged before you arrived.

Sorting by surface builds narrow rooms.
Privilege runs deep as aquifer.
The less fortunate climb
stairs that move.

Misfortune cycles like weather
no one claims to own
yet everyone defends.

Inequality wins when witnesses quit.
Collective kin is not slogan.
It is work.

Fight for right means rebuild the scale —
fairness, kindness, difference celebrated
without requiring difference to perform gratitude.

Grand world is not too late.
It is not guaranteed.
Hand in hand is tool, not photo.

Disparity removed is horizon, not arrival.

The beam still tips. You still put hands on it.
