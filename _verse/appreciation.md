Appreciation is not a performance review
for the universe.

It is the moment you name
what held you —
the hand, the meal, the accidental grace —
without converting it into debt.

Impact is quiet:
breath loosens,
shoulders drop one degree,
the day becomes survivable.

Profound is a word people use
when they are afraid of saying thank you
without a thesis attached.

The shoulders drop one degree further.
