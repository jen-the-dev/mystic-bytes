Color is not decoration.
It is want
with a wavelength.

Vermilion: appetite.
Cobalt: the breath you take
after the argument.
Every hue is a confession
the mouth hasn't earned yet.

Mixing pigments is intimacy.
You learn what bleeds
into what.

Paint the world bright
if you can afford the stain.

The palette outlasts the canvas.
