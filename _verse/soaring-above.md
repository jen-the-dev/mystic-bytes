From above, roads look like sentences
someone abandoned mid-thought.
The bird knew that already.
I learn it late,
neck tilted,
jealous without admitting it.

Soaring is not freedom forever.
It is altitude on loan —
gust accepted,
trust in wing,
landing still required.

Perspective is the payment.

The bird descends before I finish my lesson.
