The lotus opens
like it has nothing to prove.

Mud on the stem —
not pretty,
not apologizing.

A frog watches
from the bank
with the expression
of someone who has seen
this before
and is not impressed.

I count the petals
the way you count
things you expect to lose.

Thirteen is old enough
to know the bloom
does not stay open
for your benefit.

The lotus closes
before anyone asks it to.
