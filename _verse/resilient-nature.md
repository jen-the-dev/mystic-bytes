Resilience in a tree
is not poetry.
It is bend without breaking,
root hold,
season after season
of weather you did not choose.

Symphony is declined.
Monument is closer —
solid,
tender in the sway,
host to green creatures
who inherit the shade.

Safeguard is homework.
Accurate is noticing
who lives because the trunk stayed.

The forest keeps its ledger in the roots.
