Tyranny is weather that owns the house.
Unity breaks locks —
fight for right to stand sovereign,
might as earned posture, not theft.

Self-reliance guides the break.
Chains fall. Expression forms —
nation strong the way healed bone is strong.

Each sure step marks the ledger.
Determination feeds forge.
Equal rights embraced;
justice lit like harbor lamp.

Shore to shore: dignity
not auctioned by surface.

Celebrate without amnesia.
Noble sacrifice stays on the invoice.

Freedom reigns where watchers do.

The chain stays off only while maintained.
