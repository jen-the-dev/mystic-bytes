Home is where the chest
stops auditioning safety.

Hearth, kitchen, tenement, mansion —
the belonging is not square footage.
It is whether you can exhale
without performing gratitude.

Aromas and laughter attach to thresholds.
So do arguments.
Both count as architecture.

Finding belonging is not a Pinterest arc.
It is returning to a door
that still opens for you
after you have been difficult.

The exhale costs something every time.
