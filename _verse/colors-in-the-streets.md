Color in New Orleans
is not backdrop.
Scarlet argues.
Cobalt negotiates.
Vermilion shouts from the square
what the mouth delayed.

Symphony is declined.
Dialect is accurate —
each block speaks saturation,
French Quarter bright,
City Park moss muted,
both true.

Palette is not decoration.
It is emotion filed in public.

The chromatic song keeps humming after dark.
