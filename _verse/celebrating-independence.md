Once a land wore tyranny like weather —
people rose with unity,
fought for right to stand sovereign
in their own names.

Self-reliance was compass, not isolation.
Chains broke. Nation formed —
strong the way scar tissue is strong.

Confidence in the step
wrote lines in the ledger
history still reads.

Determination fed the forge.
Equal rights were not gift.
They were demand with receipts.

From shore to shore the message spread —
dignity not negotiable by surface.

Celebrate is not amnesia.
Remember the price:
blood on the invoice,
hope not refunded.

Freedom reigns where vigilance does.

The chain stays broken only if tended.
