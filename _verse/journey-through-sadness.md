Sadness removes the light without asking.
The room obeys.
Grief and sorrow keep office hours
longer than the appointment requested.

The text does not sell a lesson plan.
It records the weight:
confusion common,
oblivion tempting,
the body tired of performing recovery.

Hope, if mentioned,
arrives as a small fact —
not a reversal,
a clock still running
past midnight.

The journey does not end on uplift.
It ends on accuracy.

The clock is still running.
