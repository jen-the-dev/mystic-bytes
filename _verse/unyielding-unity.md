Unyielding is not brittle.
It bends and returns —
fraternity loyal in view,
solidarity that answers.

Bond deeper than skin.
Never alone is logistics, not poetry.

Hand in hand through thick and thin.
Hope guides; fear comments without veto.

Torch in heart tended, not performed.
Eternity is declined.
Duty is accurate.

Strength beyond length — load-bearing.
Time severs only what was decorative.

Cherish by showing up.
Ascend is declined.
Stand until the standing teaches.

Love and loyalty until end is promise
kept in ordinary hours.

The bond still holds. You still yield to each other, not to fear.
