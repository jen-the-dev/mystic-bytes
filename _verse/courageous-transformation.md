Cowardice is not a personality type.
It is fear given tenure —
hiding behind walls and objects,
dreading the ordinary challenge.

Spineless is an insult, not a diagnosis.
The body knows panic accurately sometimes.

Shame arrives with the label.
It does not always teach.
Often it rents the same room as fear.

Change is possible without erasing history.
Courage is stepwork:
forward while dread keeps commenting,
forward anyway.

Strength renews in small units —
not divine conversion,
not shining heart poster,
bravery lifting what fear weighed down.

Fear may linger.
It does not get the veto
unless you keep handing it the pen.

Face the world without requiring fearlessness first.

The step still forward. Fear still rides — not drives.
