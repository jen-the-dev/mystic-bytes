Paradise is always one more turn —
Eden on the map,
Elysium in the book,
nirvana in the song,
worries kept at bay
until they are not.

Utopia is brochure copy.
Accurate is longing:
the ache that makes you walk,
the joy that visits,
the return ticket
still in your pocket.

Golden beam is declined.
Walking is accurate.

The dream still points past the last hill.
