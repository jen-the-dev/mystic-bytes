The sky does not send cherubs.
It sends watchers at the crossing —
women with torches
who have seen this episode before.

Their love is not soft.
It is attendance:
present while you break your own rules,
present while you pretend
the breaking was fate.

Infinite love is a marketing line.
What you get is the hound's patience
and the key that fits
only the door you feared.

Thank them by staying awake.

The torch smoke stains the ceiling.
