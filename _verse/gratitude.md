Gratitude in New Orleans
is not a journal prompt.
It is after —
after kindness,
after hurricane,
after the meal
someone stayed to help you finish.

Thanks rises with jazz
because joy here
always knows its opposite.

Beacon is brochure copy.
Accurate is warmth
that remains
when the trial is still
in the room.

The thanks keeps rising after the set ends.
