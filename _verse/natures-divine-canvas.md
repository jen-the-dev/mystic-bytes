Sky does not sermonize.
It changes color
and expects you to notice
without applause.

Moss on stone —
not blessing,
just patience
with slower clocks.

The canvas is not asking
to be called divine.
You are the one
who needs a word
for being moved
without being saved.

Thirteen is old enough
to like the painting
and distrust the frame.

The moss keeps its own time.
