Horizon is not promise.
It is edge —
line where today stops
pretending it knows tomorrow.

Destiny in fate is declined.
Mold and create is accurate:
small choices,
open heart,
future as clay
not contract.

Love conquers all is brochure copy.
Try is accurate.

The line still moves when you walk toward it.
