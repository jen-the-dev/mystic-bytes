Faith is not a finish line.
It is the path you keep taking
when evidence files a dissent.

Toward is the honest word —
not inside,
not done,
still moving through doubt
like weather.

Hecate meets you mid-walk,
not at the ribbon.
She asks what you are willing to lose
and waits through the silence
you call spiritual.

Arrival is a brochure concept.
The journey keeps your shoes dirty.

The dirt is still on the soles.
