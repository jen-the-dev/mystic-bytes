Color is not decoration only.
Vermilion files appetite.
Cobalt files breath.
Rainbow is argument
that contrast can coexist
without resolving.

Thirteen learns pigment
on skin, leaf, sky —
not moral,
just signal.

Cherish is accurate.
Paint the world bright
if you can afford the stain.

The rainbow still arcs after the rain stops.
