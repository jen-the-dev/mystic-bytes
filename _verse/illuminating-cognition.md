Intelligence in New Orleans
is not quiet.
Tulane argues with the club next door.
Tremé history enters the essay.
Spoken word files dissent
in meter the parade understands.

Cognition here is syncopated —
prism, not pedestal.

Museums and galleries matter.
So does the corner opinion
that costs you nothing
and saves you years.

The mind blooms where the city
refuses to stay simple.

The sentence still swings after class ends.
