Love in New Orleans
keeps time with brass.
Soft jazz on the corner,
kiss that borrows the downbeat,
French Quarter glance
filed as permanent
until morning objects.

Eternal tune is brochure copy.
Accurate is pulse —
zydeco wild,
sultry night,
body choosing the same name
while the parade passes.

Enchantment is not trick only.
It is city weather
you wear inside the embrace.

The horn still plays for the next couple.
