In New Orleans humility keeps soft hours —
overlooked, load-bearing,
power that does not audition.

Modesty leads without billboard.
Reserve is force unconcealed
when ego would prefer thunder.

No spotlight hunger.
Place is found by walking useful.

Grace and kindness extend the hand —
unity built, not announced.

Contrition mends bridges
ego cracked.
Weave is declined.
Thread is accurate.

True richness is spirit unbound —
joy unspoken, peace without performance.

Quiet acts beacon.
Modesty guides the light.

The glow still rests. You still walk inside it.
