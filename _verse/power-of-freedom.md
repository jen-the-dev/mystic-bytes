Independence is posture.
Autonomy is schedule, not quote.

Opportunity knocks for answerers.
Liberation gives wings with weather limits.

Possibilities endless; choice priceless.
Explore passion; pay bills anyway.

Freedom is feeling alive —
power to revise the self,
prosperity not bundled.

Embrace liberty as maintenance:
cherish, protect, celebrate
with vigilance included.

Gift is tended, not owned.

The wings still work. You still guard the opening.
