Blessing is not a scoreboard.
It is bread on the table
when the week was not merciful.

Bounty tempts you to perform abundance
for people who are still hungry.
Count what is real:
the friend, the roof, the body
that carried you through the argument.

Leave room in the ledger
for what has not been fixed.

Gratitude without audit is propaganda.

The bread cools on the table.
