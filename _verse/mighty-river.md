The river does not negotiate.
It carries New Orleans
the way memory carries grudge —
steady, heavy, scenic
from a distance.

Bridges pretend unity.
The current files dissent.

Cross at your own mythology.
Triumph is optional.
Understanding is the toll.

Harmony is what you hear
when you stop fighting
the pull long enough
to listen.

The river keeps the ledger open.
