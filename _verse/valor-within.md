Courage does not climb mountains on command.
It returns the call.
It stands in the kitchen
after bad news
and washes one dish.

Fortitude, in this account,
is not lion-hearted branding.
It is the body choosing not to flee
when fleeing would be cheaper.

Pluck is a word for novels.
What the day requires
is smaller:
show up,
name the fear,
do the next accurate thing.

The dish dries on the rack.
