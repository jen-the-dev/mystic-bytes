Midnight is not Wonderland.
It is blackness with punctuation —
stars too far to borrow,
moonlight on the face
making you look like someone else.

Constellations are stories
people needed
before they had screens.

I marvel because
the scale corrects me.
Guiding light is not advice.
It is reminder:
you are small,
still awake,
still accountable to morning.

The sky keeps gliding after you blink.
