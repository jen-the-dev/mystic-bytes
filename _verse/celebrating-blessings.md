Celebration is not amnesia set to music.
It is naming what held
while the year kept its teeth.

Express the blessing plainly —
the meal, the bridge, the person
who answered —
without turning gratitude into performance
for people still outside the room.

Blessings shared honestly
make room for the unblessed hour
that is also true.

The music stops.
The table remains.
