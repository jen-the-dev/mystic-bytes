Majestic is what you say
after the current tests you.
Rapids boil.
Rocks object.
Bridge pretends connection
while the water files appeal.

Triumph on the other side
is wet evidence,
not character award.

Unstoppable is not moral.
It is observation.

The river carves whether you are watching.
