Dreams do not always fly.
Sometimes they hover —
picture in the head,
future sketched in pencil,
heart voting yes
while the feet stay on linoleum.

Flight is borrowed altitude.
Landing is part of the deal.

Coloring the world is accurate.
Never looking back is declined.

The kite still tugs the string after you wake.
