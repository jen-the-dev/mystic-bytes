Hope is not a warranty.
It is the decision to cook tomorrow
while the burn is still fresh.

Dreams arrive dressed as destinations.
Most are appetites
that forgot to introduce themselves.

Embrace both with inventory:
what you want,
what it will cost,
who pays if you fail in public.

The old blessing said arrive.
The honest blessing says revise.

Tomorrow's pot is already on the stove.
