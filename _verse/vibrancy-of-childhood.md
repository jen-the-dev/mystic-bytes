Childhood vibrates —
toddlers learning play,
offspring in every configuration,
footing negotiated daily.

Children of every color meet the same whirls
with unequal wind at their backs.
Uniqueness is not a campaign slogan.
It is how each story starts.

Foundling, firstborn, youngster at the gate —
each draft of human
with its own appetite and fear.

Watch them grow into youth
without requiring them to be symbols first.

Future in good hands is hope stated plainly,
not destiny sold.

Cherish by witnessing —
learn, explore, adore the moment
without embalming it for display.

Vibrancy is presence, not guarantee.

The footing still forms. You still watch it happen.
