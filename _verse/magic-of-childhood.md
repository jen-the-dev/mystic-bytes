Childhood is not a brand campaign.
It is young ones learning footing —
toddlers, firstborns, foundlings,
each story a different draft of human.

Kids of every color navigate the same whirls
with unequal wind at their backs.
Individuality is not decoration.
It is how the future arrives plural.

Watch them explore without requiring them
to be symbols before they are people.

Cherish the moment without embalming it.
Magic here is observation, not prophecy:
hands in dirt, question asked too loud,
future implied, not guaranteed.

Good hands is hope stated plainly —
not destiny sold,
responsibility noticed.

Adore is allowed.
So is realism.

The footing still forms. You still witness it forming.
