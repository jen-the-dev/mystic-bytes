Fame is shared hunger —
admiration in the hallways,
legacy not anecdote,
forgotten the haunting game.

Renown hard to attain;
respect that collects legions
and invoices the soul.

Delicate flame: joy, sorrow, same wick.
Wealth stain moralizes glare.
Popularity tolls heavy.

Ambition simmers beneath spirit —
warmth borrowed from strangers
who cannot know you.

Dual edges means both cut.
Tread knowing the blade faces you too.

The flame still burns both ways. You still choose proximity.
