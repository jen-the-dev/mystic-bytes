The quest is not uplink.
It is standing at the crossing
long enough for the body
to admit it is lonely
without calling loneliness holy.

Connection arrives as pressure change —
the room knows you are telling the truth
before your mouth agrees.

Hecate does not merge souls.
She holds the torch
while two people decide
whether honesty is worth the tariff.

Divine is a word for what survives
the first argument.

The pressure changes again.
