Perspective is not wisdom.
It is the angle your hunger chose
before you called it truth.

Narrow, broad, reframed —
each vista is a flirtation
with what you were willing to see.

Step back is good advice
and rarely free.
Selective is not a flaw.
It is the bill.

The lens does not widen on command.
It widens when the body
finally admits what it wanted.

The angle still belongs to you.
