The river does not explain itself.
It moves —
rapids for emphasis,
rocks for punctuation,
bridge overhead pretending connection
while the water files dissent.

Crossing is not triumph only.
It is wet receipt:
feet reminded
who owns the direction.

Thirteen is old enough
to admire force
without calling it friendly.

The current keeps carving after you leave the bank.
