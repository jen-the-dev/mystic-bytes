A figure on the road without known abode —
friend, foe, or weather passing through.

Guest in the land, hand extended,
purpose wound tight
like thread you cannot yet name.

Migrant seeks different.
Visitor from far
may spark conflict or gift
depending on who answers the door.

Acquaintance not yet made.
Story cascades in fragments.

Fate of the stranger is not yours to seal.
Brief stay or long intrigue —
both teach the same lesson:
your categories leak.

They may remain mystery.
They may become ally.
Either way the road widens
when you stop requiring strangers
to arrive pre-approved.

The figure still walks. You still revise the view.
