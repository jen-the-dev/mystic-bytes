Dream is not trance only.
It is mental picture with teeth —
fancy, spark, something to share
that may also fail in public.

Future hoped is blood paid forward.
Reach, take what is said,
make true with labor, not wish.

Magical journey is declined.
Accurate is me and you
with receipts and deadlines.

Faith in will is not innocence.
Thrill is appetite named.
Leap trusts the heart
knowing the heart can be wrong.

Leave shadows without pretending
shadows were never there.
Never looking back is brochure talk.
Look back long enough to learn.
Then forward.

Paint new art.
Unleash potential —
flight with wings that can tire.

The dream still pulls. You still leap with eyes open.
