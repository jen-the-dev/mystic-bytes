Life calls without a motto.
The body answers on schedule —
hunger, deadline, grief,
the ordinary summons.

Seize-the-day language
is for people selling posters.
This account records smaller urgencies:
answer the message,
drink water,
do not confuse velocity with meaning.

Passion is not required daily.
Attendance is.

Death is mentioned as fact, not threat.
The call continues until it doesn't.

The message light stays on.
