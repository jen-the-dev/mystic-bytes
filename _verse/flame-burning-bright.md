Love burns bright
because someone pays the fuel —
touch, kiss, return,
the body choosing the same name again.

Adoration is not innocence.
It is appetite
with better lighting.

Never fade is a song lyric.
Accurate is rekindling
after the fight,
or not rekindling
and calling that truth.

Happy place is a room you rent.
Check the lease.

The fuel gauge reads lower than morning promised.
