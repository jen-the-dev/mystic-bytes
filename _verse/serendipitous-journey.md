Serendipity is magnificent and unscheduled —
kismet with work boots,
circumstance divine or not,
surprise either way.

Happenstance charms without promising safety.
Unknown fate guides
toward better you cannot caption yet.

Gift, yes.
Not manager.

Twists craft path you did not storyboard.
Do not forget the other partner:
action, choice, the heart's labor.

Open arms is not passivity.
It is staying awake
when the plot detours.

Cosmic flow is pretty phrase for attention
meeting opportunity while pride pauses.

Bloom like flowers is declined.
Accurate is continue
with luck noted and work repeated.

The path still twists. You still steer through it.
