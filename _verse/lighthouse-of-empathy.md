Empathy is not fusion.
It is learning the weather
in someone else's body
without colonizing the forecast.

The lighthouse does not sail the ship.
It burns at its own expense
so the other vessel
can name the rocks.

You will confuse empathy with rescue.
The distinction is labor:
stay present,
do not steal the helm.

Smoke in your lungs
is the receipt for paying attention.

The lamp warms your hands.
