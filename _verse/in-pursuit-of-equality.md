Unfairness shows its face.
Injustice fills dread accurately
when the door was narrow before you grew.

Sorting builds opposition.
Privilege aquifer feeds the top floor.
Pace kept by those
the system excluded by design.

Misfortune cycles — mystery sold,
nothing and everything
explained by the same rigged scale.

Inequality triumphs when witnesses quit.
Shared humanity is labor, not lyric.

Fight for just means rebuild brightness
where fairness and kindness prevail
and difference is not required to audition gratitude.

Hatred-free world is horizon.
Magnificent is declined.
Honest is enough.

Hand in hand — tool, not photo.

The stair still moves. You still climb together.
