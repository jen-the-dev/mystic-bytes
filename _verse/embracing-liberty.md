Independence is posture before policy —
autonomy lived, not quoted.

Opportunity knocks for those who answer.
Liberation gives wings
that still obey weather.

Range of possibilities is real.
Choice is priceless
because it costs.

Explore passion without requiring passion
to pay every bill.

Freedom is feeling alive
with power to revise the self —
prosperity not guaranteed,
responsibility included.

Cherish, protect, celebrate —
verbs with maintenance schedules.

Gift is not permanent.
It is tended.

The wings still spread. You still tend the liberty.
