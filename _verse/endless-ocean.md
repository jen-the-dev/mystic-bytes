Endless is not comfort.
It is the ocean refusing closure —
waves from offing,
creatures you cannot name,
magenta dusk
writing on water
you cannot keep.

Wonder is not safety.
Freedom on a wave
is loaned.

Mystery is accurate.
Priceless is declined.

The heart increases its questions at the shoreline.
