Enchantment is a surface condition —
light on water,
the room believing you
before it knows your name.

Blessing is slower:
what keeps you alive
when the charm expires.

Do not confuse the sparkle
with the covenant.
Hecate's enchantments
include the dog, the keys,
the hour you cannot sweet-talk.

Charm fades on schedule.
The covenant asks for return visits.

The sparkle drains by morning.
