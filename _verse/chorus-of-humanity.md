Humanity is chorus, not single note —
individuals from every register,
flaws and strengths in polyphony.

Civility foundations conduct.
Compassion is not decoration.
Authenticity sought by repair.

Common ground found in difference
when sorting gate is refused.

Joint endeavor builds peace —
horizon, not arrival.

Tapestry is declined.
Thread is accurate.

Uplift without drowning the quiet voice.
Joy and love as fuel, not poster.

The chorus still widens. You still keep time.
