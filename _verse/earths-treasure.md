Treasure in earth
is not jewelry brochure.
It is sediment —
time pressed into stone,
metal as patience,
gem as accident
you rename sacred
because you dug.

Fortune favors bold is half true.
Accurate is respect:
peek,
listen,
do not confuse extraction
with worth.

The trove keeps its older receipts underground.
