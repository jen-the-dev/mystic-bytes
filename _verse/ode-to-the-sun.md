Ode is too formal for friendship.
The sun is not friend.
It is fuel.

Winter remembers the bill.
Summer presents it daily.
Shadow is the discount
you earn by standing aside.

Thank the heavens is declined.
Thank the orbit.

The star keeps burning without your praise.
