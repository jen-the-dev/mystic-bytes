Limitless is brochure talk.
Potential is real
and still bounded by fuel, frame, time.

Foresight turns promise into action
or into décor. Vision without motion
is wallpaper.

Hazard is friction test —
plausible risk, shot taken,
all you've got meaning
what you are willing to lose.

Each step unfolds a corridor
not every corridor is virtue.

Destiny is clay, not crown.
Creativity shapes future
with nerve and repetition.

Beautiful realization is declined.
Honest completion is enough.

Embrace possibilities
without requiring them to be infinite.

The door still opens. You still walk through with limits named.
