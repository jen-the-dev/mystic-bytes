Time dances
the way a streetcar dances —
arrives late,
leaves anyway,
you on the step or not.

New Orleans teaches:
second passes,
memory attaches —
beignet sugar,
river sound,
oak shade,
all filed fast.

Cherish is not poster language.
Accurate is notice
before the bite is gone.

The chime still marks the next second.
