Summer charges interest on the skin.
Sand sticks.
Waves knock the knees.
You stay anyway because
the body voted yes
before the mind filed objections.

Retreat is not escape only.
It is noticing
how loud the quiet becomes
when the season allows laziness.

Dog days are honest:
joy and sweat share a towel.

The breeze still arrives from the water after sundown.
