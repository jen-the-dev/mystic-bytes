Bean dreamed liqueur — sweet, smooth, far.
Race long; heat first.

Roast: keep seat or lose the dream.
Brew, filter: spell not finished.

Sugar, cream, steam dream —
stirred hours until heart mends into pour.

Perfect liqueur is not innocence.
It is ordeal translated to treat.

Struggling bean carries bright show
only if it survives the heat.

Know the glow is earned.

The bean still transforms. The glass still waits.
