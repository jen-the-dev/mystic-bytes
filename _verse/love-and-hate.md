Hate is not the opposite of love on a poster.
It is the same heat
with a different target selected.

Wrath keeps excellent records.
So does want.

The duel is not heroic.
It is two animals
in one chest
arguing over who gets the next hour.

Love does not conquer by slogan.
It wins only when it stops performing purity
and admits the cost.

Debris remains either way.

The kitchen still smells of both.
