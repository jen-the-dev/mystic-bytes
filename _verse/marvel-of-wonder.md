Wonder is not a poster.
It is the jolt
when the world
does something you did not script —
bug on the step,
book that opens wrong,
question you cannot put down.

Thunder in the rules means stay awake.
Fresh means the next page
still owes you an answer.

The unknown keeps its door cracked.
