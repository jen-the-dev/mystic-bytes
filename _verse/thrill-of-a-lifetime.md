Lifetime thrill is not safe.
It is night climb,
unknown voyage,
mountain goal,
heart loud
before the mind signs.

Joy and grace is brochure copy.
Accurate is pulse —
the chase still running
when the treasure
was never the point.

Leap is real.
Landing is also real.

The chase keeps its rhythm after you stop.
