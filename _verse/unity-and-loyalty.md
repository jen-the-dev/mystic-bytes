Comradeship is not scenery.
It is weight shared —
fraternity that keeps the room
from collapsing into solo weather.

Bond beyond skin and bone
means someone answers
when the hour goes ugly.

Side by side is not performance.
It is logistics of survival:
hope as lamp oil, not poster.

Trials arrive. Fear comments.
You persevere because the bond
is load-bearing, not decorative.

Torch carried is responsibility —
flame you tend so the next walker
sees the stair.

Ascend is declined.
Accurate is stand together
until the standing teaches something.

The torch still burns. You still carry it in company.
