Triumph is thin air
and a view
that makes the valley
look like a story
you used to live.

Every step is tax —
crag, precipice,
courage borrowed,
lungs protesting.

Free at the top
lasts one breath.
Then descent
files its own invoice.

The mountain keeps its majesty after you leave the peak.
