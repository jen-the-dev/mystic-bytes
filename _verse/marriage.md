Marriage is not a finale.
It is courtship
with paperwork and plumbing.

The ring is a symbol.
The work is dishes,
sickness,
desire retained
when familiarity tries to file it away.

Alliance sounds noble.
Accurate is choosing the same person
on a Tuesday
when nobody is photographing it.

Exploring ends where maintenance begins.
That is not less romantic.
It is more expensive.

The ring catches light. The sink catches everything.
