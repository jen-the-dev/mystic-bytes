Tapestry is not fabric here.
It is field and sky
stitched by attention —
hue arguing with hue,
light cutting shape,
time paused one frame
in a world that won't hold still.

Masterpiece is declined.
Accurate is looking long enough
for the heart to file a copy
it cannot reproduce.

Divine love is brochure copy.
Majesty is what you notice
when the brush is weather.

The colors keep dancing after you blink.
