Forbidden is a markup strategy.
The body shops there anyway.

Temptation does not need your consent form.
It needs your pulse.

Resistance is not purity theater.
It is choosing which invoice
you can survive wearing
when the room finds out.

Some battles are lost in public.
Some are won in private
and still cost the same.

Steel yourself is a slogan.
Accurate is slower:
name the want,
name the price,
decide if the soul is for sale.

The price tag is still on the wrist.
