Music unites without asking permission —
melody in the chest, rhythm binding,
joy and harmony from the first beat.

Birdsong, instruments, hymns soft —
hands clap because the body insists.

Orchestra is accurate.
Symphony is declined.

Nature backdrop, sweet tunes as memory
locked not by secrecy but by attention.

Poetry and storytelling in sound.
Universal feeling, delight, solace, grace.

Carries away from pain briefly —
not erases, relocates.

Ever-evolving art nothing replaces.
Join the chorus again when ready.

The chorus still calls. You still answer.
