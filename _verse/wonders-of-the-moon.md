The moon does not need ode language.
It waxes because it does.
Ocean answers.
I notice from the window,
not from a textbook.

Apollo on the ground
does not make the sphere domestic.
It makes the distance louder.

Pearl is pretty.
Accurate is pull —
something in you
feeling tug
without consent or explanation.

Divine design is declined.
Observable is enough.

The crescent still files its report on the water.
