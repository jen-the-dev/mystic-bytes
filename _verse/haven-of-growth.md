Growth in New Orleans
is not a seminar.
It is oak logic —
roots in questionable soil,
branches anyway,
storm as pruning,
rebuild as ritual.

Haven is not safe.
It is fertile and inconvenient.
Obstacles become footing
when the city refuses
your small story.

Blossoming is not guaranteed.
Attempt is.

The bud still opens toward the unreliable sun.
