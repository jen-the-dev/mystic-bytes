Eternity does not stay for photos.
It passes —
blink,
glance,
heartbeat with a stranger,
room changed
before you name it.

Volumes without words is accurate.
Magic forever is declined.

A glimpse is enough
to prove time has teeth
and still lets you keep something.

The blink still files its receipt after you look away.
