Freedom in New Orleans
is not a poster.
It is laissez-faire with consequences —
second line in the street,
path carved between obligations,
indigo sky,
brass in the blood.

Untamed does not mean careless.
It means the city refuses
your small definition
of who you ought to become.

Wings are brochure copy.
Accurate is moving
while the horns insist
you are still alive.

The second line still claims the asphalt.
