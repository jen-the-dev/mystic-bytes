Creativity is not a virtue post.
It is ember — small, insistent,
refusing to credit your schedule.

You feel it in the wrists first:
the itch to make
before you have permission
or a clean desk.

Some nights it is warmth.
Some nights arson at the edge of the page.

Feed it or fear it —
both are forms of attention.

The ash remembers what you tried to name.
