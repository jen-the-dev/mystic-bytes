Science fiction is a door
left open on purpose —
aliens as question,
robots as mirror,
black hole as punctuation
for adults who forgot
how big maybe is.

Fear is allowed.
So is going anyway.

Imagination is not escape only.
It is rehearsal.

The ship still leaves dock after you close the book.
