Innocence is not armor.
It is light
before the body learns
what people charge for access.

Naive is not insult here.
It is the brief season
when touch still meant comfort
and not negotiation.

Protect it if you can.
Not because it lasts —
it doesn't —
but because the memory of unguarded warmth
teaches the heart
what to miss accurately.

Eternity is declined.
Luminance is enough.

The light dims. You keep the receipt.
