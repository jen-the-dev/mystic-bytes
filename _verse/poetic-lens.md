Perspective in New Orleans
is not neutral.
French Quarter angle,
river angle,
balcony iron framing grief
and appetite equally.

Tapestry is labor.
Street musician,
painter, spoken word —
each lens files a different truth
about the same humidity.

Masterpiece is declined.
Accurate is overlap:
you see more
when you admit
what you wanted to find.

The lens shifts. The humidity stays.
