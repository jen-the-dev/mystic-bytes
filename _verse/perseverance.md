Perseverance is not a race won on camera.
It is the next step taken
when the odds are already documented
against the body.

Courage, in this account,
does not throw gauntlets.
It returns to the task
after the task has humiliated you once.

Trials arrive in different uniforms.
The response remains similar:
stay present,
refuse the neat collapse,
accept the road may stay rough
longer than the brochure promised.

Triumph is not scheduled here.
The step is.
