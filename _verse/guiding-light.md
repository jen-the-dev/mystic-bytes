Dark times do not send angels
with customer service voices.

They send the torch
that shows the stairs
you have been pretending
are not yours to climb.

Guiding light is not comfort.
It is enough visibility
to choose the next harm
or the next honesty.

Hecate does not walk you out.
She walks beside you
until you stop asking to be carried.

The wick is shorter than you hoped.
