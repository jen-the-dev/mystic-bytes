I am building a life between Louisiana and Aotearoa — remote work, relocation paperwork, and the ordinary logistics of staying online while the hemisphere changes. These notes are for builders who travel, not influencers performing travel.

**Focus:** How to Navigate New Horizons- Building Your.

Verify postal rules for packages and banking mail. Build a portable desk kit: stand, mouse, cable pouch. Client comms from Oceania to US/EU need explicit response windows — under-promise, over-document.
